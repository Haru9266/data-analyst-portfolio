Bank fraud detection analysis

6.3M+ Transactions · Python, SQL & Power BI

End-to-end fraud detection system analyzing 6.3 million+ banking transactions — from raw data to automated fraud alerts.

Key Insight Fraud occurs only in CASH_OUT and TRANSFER transactions (8,213 cases, 0.13%). The bank's own flagging system caught just 16 of those 8,213 cases. 98% of fraud cases leave the account balance at zero — a strong red-flag pattern.

Business Impact Highlights major gaps in the bank's existing fraud detection system and gives a low-cost, automated way to catch fraud earlier and reduce losses.
